Projects

Projects:
Travel Guide of Turkey

I created a website that helps users plan their vacation or travel plans to the beautiful country of Turkiye. The site contains multiple pages like Culture, History, Cuisine, and Upcoming events. Each page has its own respective content about the wonders of Turkey which allows users to easily learn more about the country and specific places to go or things to do/try.

Restaurant Ordering System

Working with a partner, we created a restaurant ordering system application in C# that included the use of a database while implementing CRUD functionality. The project was created to look and feel professional. It had a login system with different types of access and permissions for different users. It allowed the creation, modification and deletion of orders and items. It also would generate an invoice for the order once completed and followed component based software design principles.

Car Service Center System

I created an application that would allow a car dealerships service center to use for their service processes. The project contains lots of functions such as creating new customer accounts and storing their personal vehicles and past appointments in their accounts. It also allows appointments to be setup and has a full list of services/repairs that can be chosen from and will also generate an invoice once the services are completed and logged into the database.
